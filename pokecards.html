Pokecards

Pokécards is a work-in-progress, minimalist card game inspired by the Pokémon franchise. Designed to be simple and accessible, it's meant to be played on the go only offering the original Generation 1 Pokémon. The game focuses on the core aspects of Pokémon battles, such as basic stats, abilities, and moves, in an easy-to-understand and quick-to-play format. Pokécards is being developed as a web app, with a release date hopefully sometime in the next few months.

Tools Used: Python, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, Figma
