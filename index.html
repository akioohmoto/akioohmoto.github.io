news

EagleRide at HackTheHeights 2024 - October 2024

I recently had the exciting opportunity to participate in HackTheHeights 2024, Boston College's annual hackathon! Over the course of the event, I worked alongside peers to develop EagleRide, a ride-sharing platform designed specifically for BC students.

During the hackathon, we focused on creating a user-friendly, secure, and efficient system for students to coordinate rides for trips, airport runs, and weekend outings.

HackTheHeights was an amazing experience that pushed my problem-solving and development skills. We're working on refining EagleRide to bring it closer to reality!
